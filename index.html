Hey there!

My name is Kelsey and I am a second year student at the University of Winchester, studying Games Design and Development.

As a child I decided that I wanted to be a game designer and set my sights on it. Now that I am studying it for my degree I can actualise that wish, and so I now strive to improve my skills so that I can create games like I wanted to do as a child!

In my free time I love playing games, often spending a lot of time playing them with friends. Alongside this I have a passion for creative writing and photography, which are both areas I love for their creative freedom.
